Bharat Group of Colleges has been founded under the aegis of Mahavir Educational & Charitable Trust (Regd.), Sardulgarh.
The Institute has been built in peaceful surroundings in village Khera Khurd which is at a distance of 4KMs. from Sardulgarh Town. The Institute is spread over 19 acres land with lush green lawns, modern buildings and infrastructure BGC, today, is a knowledge powerhouse and a brand name in higher education.
The Institution is approved by AICTE, Punjab Govt. and Affiliated to Maharaja Ranjit Singh Punjab Technical University, Bathinda.
BGC is ISO 9001-2008 certified. The Institute aims at providing technical & Professional education to the students so that they may get gainful employment after completion of their study.
The main thrust of the Institute is to develop the professional skills of the students so that they may face the tough competition in their life.
